Public Sector
DEFRA — Border Trade Matching Service
Via Equal Experts

Public Sector
DEFRA — IPAFFS (Import of Products, Animals, Food and Feed System)
Via Kainos / Anson McCade
Product Consultant and Business Analyst on IPAFFS — the service managing the import of regulated goods into the UK. Delivered EU harmonisation of plant risk levels integrating with Microsoft Dynamics 365, managed commodity code updates to CN2022 and CN2024 standards, and led the business analysis function on the Common User Charge implementation including integration design using industry-standard cloud tooling. Operated within a GDS delivery approach, managing the product backlog in Jira with documentation in Confluence.
Public Sector
Department for Education — Cloud Infrastructure Platform
Via Methods
Lead IT Business Analyst and Product Owner on the CIP programme, establishing a new Microsoft Azure-based platform for consumption across DfE services. Led requirements capture for the DfE API Management Service, owning the product backlog in Azure DevOps and successfully taking the service through a GDS assessment against the Service Standard. Also led requirements capture for an Azure-hosted integration middleware solution connecting Zendesk, ServiceNow, and Dynamics 365 CRM via APIs, Logic Apps, Function Apps, and Service Bus using a pub/sub model.
Private Sector
Calyx — Technology Separation Programme
Via M3 Consulting
Lead Business Analyst on an M&A programme to legally and financially separate Calyx — a global provider of medical imaging and eClinical software — from its former parent company, Parexel. Led analysis of Microsoft Azure resource utilisation across the organisation, engaged directly with Microsoft to transfer subscription ownership between Enterprise Agreements, and coordinated globally distributed development teams through application migration.
Private Sector
Rolls-Royce

IT Business Analyst across multiple workstreams within the Business Aviation, Civil Aerospace, and Defence divisions. Delivered requirements capture for a replacement Technical Publications solution, led the migration of an on-premise Maximo instance to an IBM Cloud-hosted SaaS deployment including RBAC implementation for third-party access, and led the digitisation of the MRO process — coordinating with an external design agency to build a front-end solution integrating with Maximo.
Private Sector
Jaguar Land Rover — Vehicle Operations Transformation

Contributed to the business case that secured programme funding, facilitated requirements workshops with business SMEs, and developed prototype wireframes to build senior stakeholder understanding and buy-in.
