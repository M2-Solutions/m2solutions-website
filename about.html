About M2 Solutions

Experienced. Practical.
Enterprise-focused.

We're a specialist consultancy that helps large organisations make sense of complex transformation challenges — and deliver against them.

Built on deep
enterprise experience.

M2 Solutions was founded to address a gap that repeatedly shows up in large-scale transformation programmes: the disconnect between what the business needs and what gets built. Too often, organisations invest in technology without the analytical rigour to ensure it solves the right problem.

We bring a structured, business-first approach to every engagement. Our starting point is always understanding the organisation — its processes, its data, and the outcomes it's trying to achieve — before any consideration of technology or tooling.

With a background spanning complex systems integration, API-led connectivity, and enterprise data strategy, we are equally at home in boardroom conversations and deep technical delivery. That range is where we add the most value.

We typically work through established consultancies and system integrators — embedding within their delivery teams to fulfil Business Analyst and Product Owner roles on client programmes. Our partners rely on us to represent them well: delivering with the seniority, reliability, and depth of understanding their own clients expect.

How we work

Principles that guide
every engagement.

Clarity first

Before recommending solutions, we invest time in understanding the problem. Clear thinking at the outset saves significant cost and effort down the line — and leads to better outcomes.

Honest counsel

We tell clients what we genuinely believe, not what's easiest to hear. Enterprise decision-makers need advisors they can trust to be direct — particularly when the stakes are high.

Practical delivery

Strategy without delivery is just documentation. We focus on outputs that are usable, implementable, and aligned with how the organisation actually works — not theoretical best practice.
